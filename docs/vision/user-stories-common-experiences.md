# Common Experiences

> **Implementation Status:** This is a design document describing the vision for universal experiences. The Experiences system is planned for future development.

## Introduction

The concept of "Common Experiences" is designed to provide a baseline for meaningful engagement in any region. These
experiences are not region-specific but are activities or interactions that can be completed in any region to enrich
your travel experience. By participating in these generic experiences, users can gauge their level of engagement and
understanding of different regions.

## List of Common Experiences

### Basic Engagement

- **Stay on the territory**: Spend time in the region to get a feel for the place.
- **Overnight**: Stay at least one night to experience the region more fully.

### Social Interaction

- **Interact with locals**
  - **Casual**: Basic interactions like asking for directions.
  - **Regular Social**: Engaging in social activities or public events.
  - **Informal Personal**: Making friends or having deeper conversations.
  - **Deep**: Establishing meaningful relationships or understanding local issues.

### Culinary Experiences

- **Taste local food**
  - **In a restaurant**: Try local dishes in a restaurant setting.
  - **Homemade**: Experience homemade or street food.

### Language and Communication

- **Learn the local alphabet**: To pronounce words and read signs.
- **Learn a list of basic phrases in the local language**: Such as "hi," "bye," "thanks," etc.
- **Use the local transportation system**: Navigate the region like a local.

### Cultural and Historical Understanding

- **Engage in cultural activities**: Attend local events, festivals, or traditional activities.
- **Visit historical landmarks**: Explore significant historical sites.
- **Learn about the local history**: Through museums, guides, or educational materials.

### Exploration and Understanding

- **Explore the natural environment**: Hike, swim, or simply enjoy the natural beauty.
- **Understand the local economy**: Learn about the main industries, markets, etc.
- **Learn about local governance**: Understand the political structure and local laws.

### Art and Social Norms

- **Discover local art and craftsmanship**: Visit galleries, workshops, or markets.
- **Understand social norms and etiquette**: Learn the do's and don'ts in social settings.

### Educational Activities

- **Visit educational or informative institutions**: Such as museums, schools, or research centers.
- **Try regional recreational activities**: Engage in local sports or outdoor activities.
- **Gain knowledge of the local flora and fauna**: Through guided tours or educational materials.

### Advanced Engagement

- **Read local literature or watch regional films**: To understand local narratives and perspectives.
- **Visit a local business (not as a customer)**: To understand the work culture.
- **Work in a region**: Engage in short-term work or volunteer activities.

By engaging in these generic experiences, users can have a well-rounded and meaningful travel experience, regardless of
the region they are visiting.
